## 💡 Tips for tomorrow's meeting

**Body language to watch for:**
- When they LIGHT UP about a SEM feature → they love it, beat it or partner with it
- When they SIGH about a SEM feature → that's our opportunity
- When they say "we work around this" → they have an unmet need
- When they say "honestly" or "to be fair" → they're about to tell you the real story

**Questions that reveal more than they ask:**
- "Walk me through your morning routine in SEM" (reveals daily pain)
- "What did you have before SEM?" (reveals what bar they're judging from)
- "If your assistant could only use ONE feature in SEM, which?" (reveals the core value)
- "What did you ALMOST buy instead of SEM?" (reveals the competitive set)
- "What would make you cancel SEM tomorrow?" (reveals the lock-in / churn driver)

**Trap questions to AVOID asking:**
- "Don't you think AI would help?" (leading)
- "Wouldn't it be better if..." (leading)
- "Most brokers tell us..." (using fake social proof)
- "We have a feature that..." (early demo before discovery)
